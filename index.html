Full Body Workout

This full body workout is designed to help you gain strength and endurance. Follow along with the exercises below:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 15 reps
